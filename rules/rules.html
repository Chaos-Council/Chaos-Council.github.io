    People who run this channal are kids, and most people on this server are also kids

    If we reject your application, it's not personal. It just means something didn’t meet the requirements. In the prof of read box in the form type "I have read the rules and will respect them"

    - Punishment: 1 day to 1 week timeout

  7. No spam or self-promotion

    This includes server invites, advertisements, etc. Only do when you get permission from an admin or mod. This includes DMing fellow members.

    - Punishment: 12 hours to 5 days timeout

  8. Use common sense

    No loop holes for anything like that.
